# Siju K S
### Associate Professor of Mathematics | Saintgits College of Engineering (Autonomous)
### Research Scholar | Amrita School of Artificial Intelligence, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Coimbatore

I am a faculty member in the Department of Mathematics at Saintgits College of Engineering with over 20 years of experience teaching pure, computational, and applied mathematics across science, engineering, and technology programs. Alongside my faculty role, I am currently a Research Scholar at the Amrita School of Artificial Intelligence, where my work bridges deep mathematical theory and cutting-edge machine learning paradigms. 

In addition to my academic roles, I serve as a Data Analytics consultant for engineering and social sciences, and as a National Resource Person for AI & ML training initiatives led by prominent institutes like IIT Madras and the Government of Kerala.

---

## 🔬 Research Interests & Works
My research addresses the intersection of mathematical modeling and deep learning, with a specialized focus on medical image restoration. I work on deblurring and reconstruction techniques for clinical imaging modalities (CT, MRI, X-ray) to bridge computational theory and practical clinical requirements. 

**Core Research Areas:**
* **Custom Deep Learning Architectures:** Developing mathematical formulations for specialized network components, including the Spatially-Adaptive Gated Activation (SAGA) and the Augmented Differential L1 (ADL1) loss function. 
* **Clinical Imaging Optimization:** Formulating physics-informed models and carefully evaluating architectural choices, such as the deployment of SiLU and ELU activation units, to enhance interpretability and accuracy in clinical contexts. 
* **Collaborative Medical AI:** Partnering directly with clinical experts to ensure models meet rigorous diagnostic standards.

**Recent Publications:**
* **EffNet-SVM:** A Hybrid Model for Diabetic Retinopathy Classification Using Retinal Fundus Images [DOI: 10.1109/ACCESS.2025.3566073](https://doi.org/10.1109/ACCESS.2025.3566073)
* **X-GAN:** Physics-informed generative model for edge-preserved chest X-ray image denoising [DOI: 10.1016/j.cmpbup.2026.100247](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cmpbup.2026.100247)
* An interpretable deep learning method for medical image deblurring and restoration [DOI: 10.1016/j.health.2026.100468](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.health.2026.100468)
